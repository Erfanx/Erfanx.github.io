How to replace bad habits with good ones?



How to replace bad habits with good ones?

An effective way to control your habits

By Syed Irfan | November 22, 2020
Habit is something that is a regular practice and hard to give up. We don’t get tensed if the habit is good. But when the habit is bad we get stressed but can’t get over it. The most important thing is to understand that both types of habit are harmful to us. For instance, you have a habit of studying 8 hours a day. No doubt the habit is good but it will not allow you to see what is above it. There are so many important things in our life than study. For example, to look after our parents. If you achieve something big in life but you don’t have a good relationship with your parents, your achievements will be valueless. So what we need to do is to control our action and use the power of habit to do great things. Don’t let your habit control your life, control your habit to make your life great.

Now the question is how it can be done. I will describe in this blog what I do to make this happen. First of all, take a notebook or a diary where you can note which will only be used for these tasks. Try to note them in pen and paper rather than typing in word files on a computer or mobile phone. Then divide a single page into two. On the left top side write “What I will do today”. In this column, you will write what you will do today. If you are doing it in the middle of the day, then note it for the next day. Take a whole day in concern to make this list for your first day. And on the right top side, write “What I did today”. In this column, you will just mark the points of what you did as you planned. I put an example here to make it more clear.

After that, the question will arrive that what should we write in the “What I will do today” column. For this, you have to sit quietly so that you can think. First, imagine the perfect day of your life on the basis of what is coming on the next day. For example, on the next day morning, after walking up you will get fresh first and then you will go to University to withdraw some documents of your graduation. So write in point no. 1 - Get Fresh, point no. 2 - Go to University to withdraw documents and gradually write everything that you are about to do in your day. List at least 15 to 20 points. You can write anything no matter how small the task is. Thus, you will complete the “What I will do today” column the day before. It will help you to visualize your whole perfect day in just one page when you wake up in the morning and see it.


So the plan is being made. On your first day when you will do your first task, mark on the right writing “Done” or put on a tick. Gradually when the number of “Done” or ticks get increased it will give you an inner calm inspiration which will motivate you to do more. At the end of the day, you can see how was your day, what mistakes you did and after that plan accordingly for the next day. Do this for 21 days straight, your habit and way of thinking will be changed.

Every small task and action will lead to making your day beautiful and every beautiful day will lead to making your life beautiful. So all the best for the new beginning of your life.

And once you understand that habits can change, you have the freedom and the responsibility to remake them. -Charles Duhigg
