The Person

Syed Md. Irfanul Alam

Also known as: Syed Irfan, Irfan Curriculum Vitae

Syed Irfan was born in Chittagong, the port city of Bangladesh. After finishing his secondary schooling from Nasirabad Govt. High School, he enrolled in Bangladesh Navy College Chittagong for higher secondary school certification. He then moved to Dhaka and finished the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science & Engineering from North South University.

He was a Software Engineer at Codephilics. During his BS, he was an official member of Toastmasters International, participated in regular programs arranged by the organization, and was certified as an active charter member of the organization. He was also a member of North South University ACM SIGAPP Student Chapters and North South University Problem Solvers.


Career Interest

Seeking a role in Machine Learning Engineer, Data Scientist, Mobile App Developer & Web Developer where the knowledge and analytical skills acquired by pursuing the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science & Engineering would be deployed. Looking for a progressive company where the skills are utilized in the maximum way possible.

Skilled in Jupyter, Python, Object-Oriented Programming, Android Studio, Java, Flutter, Bootstrap, JavaScript, MySQL, MS Excel, and Critical Thinking.


Research Interest

The primary research interest is to ease the problems of the developing societies and empower people for better lifestyle. The primary field of interest is Artificial Intelligence (AI). Within AI, his interest includes Machine Learning, Deep Learning & Artificial Neural Network.
