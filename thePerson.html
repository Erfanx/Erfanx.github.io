The Person




About Syed Irfan

Syed Irfan was born in Chittagong, the port city of Bangladesh. Grew up in Chittagong, he started the kindergarten education at Saint Lawrence Academy in the year 2000. In the 6th standard he got admitted into Central Public School & College and studied there till the 8th standard. Next he attended the admission test in Nasirabad Government High School and obtained 5th place amongst 721 candidates in 2009. After finishing his secondary schooling (2010-2012) from Nasirabad Government High School, he enrolled in Bangladesh Navy College Chittagong for higher secondary school certification (2012-2014). He then moved to Dhaka and finished the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science & Engineering (2015-2020) from North South University.

He was a Software Engineer at Codephilics, a software startup company of one of his friends. Being a part of a startup company he faced the struggle a company faces in the beginning and grew up through obstacles. At the time of pursuing his bachelor's degree he was an official member of Toastmasters International, participated in regular programs arranged by the organization, and was certified as an active charter member of the organization. He was also a member of North South University ACM SIGAPP Student Chapters and North South University Problem Solvers.


Career Summary

Self-motivated and passionate individual with a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science and Engineering, professional experience in developing mobile application, machine learning model, web scraper and software projects. Interested in the role of Data Scientist, Mobile Application Development and Machine Learning Engineer. Have the ability to analyze business models efficiently and make data driven decisions for better outcomes. Skilled in Python, Java, Object Oriented Programming, Flutter, MySQL etc.


Research Interest

The primary goal of research is to ease the problems of developing societies and empower people for better lifestyles. The primary field of interest is Artificial Intelligence (AI). Within AI, his interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning & Artificial Neural Network.
